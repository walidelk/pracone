THE IMPACT OF SOCIAL MEDIA ON THE MUSIC INDUSTRY

The internet continues to revolutionize just about everything we do—including within the music industry.

The Industry Has Changed

Musicians need to understand that the music industry has changed. In the past, musicians would need to do things like record demos and distribute them to fans. They needed to bargain for the time in the studio. If there were live shows at local venues—acts would need to find any opportunity to play.
